Services Offered

Cleaning and Janitorial Services

Our maintenance programs commonly entail the following services: restroom cleaning and disinfecting, trash gathering, collection, removal and recycling, stairs and common area cleaning, sweeping, mopping and polishing of floors, entrance, reception area and foyer cleaning, wipe and disinfect doorknobs, telephones and light switches, kitchen and lounge area including sinks, counter tops, microwaves, toasters, and refrigerators. Horizontal surface dusting, floor dusting, wet mop and machine scrubbing, high dusting, stripping and refinishing, air condition vent cleaning, sanitizing all lavatory fixtures, sinks, partitions, walls, etc.. vacuuming, window washing and blind cleaning, cleaning of light fixtures, baseboards, millwork, etc.


Proposal

Dynasty Office Cleaning Services, fully licensed, bonded and insured, will perform an on-site survey to better understand and discuss the clients' precise needs. After the on-site survey, we will provide you with a customized "Cleaning Proposal". Your customized proposal will simply out line your cleaning service needs based on your size and budget. If the client agrees and accepts Dynasty as their service provider, our priority from then on is client satisfaction by performing the best job possible. Our neatly dressed uniformed personnel always provide premium service for your company while maintaining the security of your office and facility.


Schedule

Commercial accounts can be maintained daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, semi-annually, or any schedule that best suits your company needs. Convenience is in your hands! For more information on our office cleaning services, you may contact us to request a free estimate. We look forward to working with you.
